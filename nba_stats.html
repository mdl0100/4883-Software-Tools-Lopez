Jamal Murray: 8 Assists

Jamal Murray dished out a game-high eight assists to go with 14 points and eight rebounds in Denver's 94-89 win over Miami in Game 5 of the NBA Finals to help the Nuggets win their first NBA championships. Murray became the fourth player to average at least 20 points and 10 assists in an NBA Finals.
